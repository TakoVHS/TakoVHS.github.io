Disclaimer

The CogniPrint Research Initiative provides statistical and heuristic analysis for research purposes only.

What CogniPrint Is

  • A mathematical framework for constructing compact statistical profiles (cognitive fingerprints) of text.
  • A set of reproducible tools for studying empirical regularities and profile similarity.
  • An open research programme aimed at scholarly publication and citation.

What CogniPrint Is Not

  • Not a definitive source classifier.
  • Not an authorship attribution or verification tool.
  • Not a forensic or legal instrument.
  • Not a product that provides guaranteed or final judgments about any text.

Interpretation of Results

All outputs, including metrics, fingerprint vectors, and heuristic assessments, are analytical signals, not conclusions. Any interpretation must remain within the context of research and experimentation.
