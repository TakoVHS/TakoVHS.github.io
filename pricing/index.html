Hosted Access

An optional convenience layer around the CogniPrint research workflow. The research core and public evidence package remain separate.

Current status

Billing integration is in test-mode preparation. This route is intentionally cautious: paid access is about hosted workflow convenience, managed analysis, and review-oriented exports, not stronger scientific conclusions.

Conceptual plans

  • Starter: individual researchers, monthly subscription, hosted analysis convenience, limited usage.
  • Research Pro: labs and independent teams, higher usage, report exports, private project workspace.
  • Institution: manual contact, review-oriented support, audit-friendly workflows.

What hosted access does not imply

Hosted access does not turn CogniPrint into a forensic system, an attribution decision engine, or a completed empirical study.
