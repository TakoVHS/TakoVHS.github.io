# Placement Plan — Where to Publish the Offers

## Goal

Publish two offers across platforms where buyers already search for technical operators, productized services, automation, AI MVP recovery and mass hiring systems.

Offers:

1. AI Repo Rescue Sprint
2. HR Autopilot

## Tier 1 — Publish first

### 1. Upwork Project Catalog

Best for: AI Repo Rescue Diagnostic and Rescue Sprint.

Why: clients can buy predefined, fixed-scope projects. Strong fit for productized diagnostics.

Publish:

- Project 1: AI Repo Rescue Diagnostic
- Project 2: AI MVP Launch Readiness Sprint

Pricing:

- Diagnostic: $750 / $1,250 / $1,500
- Sprint: custom quote after diagnostic

### 2. Contra

Best for: premium portfolio and direct international clients.

Why: portfolio + project workflow + direct payment flow.

Publish:

- Service: AI Repo Rescue Sprint
- Service: Telegram Automation / HR Autopilot Systems

### 3. LinkedIn Services Page

Best for: founder and B2B credibility.

Why: services are attached to the personal profile and discoverable through LinkedIn/search.

Publish:

- Service Page headline: AI-first technical operator
- Services: Custom Software Development, IT Consulting, Business Consulting, SaaS Development, Automation

### 4. TenChat

Best for: Russian-speaking B2B, HR, entrepreneurs, mass hiring.

Publish:

- Post 1: HR Autopilot for mass hiring
- Post 2: AI Repo Rescue for founders and teams
- Profile service: Telegram automation and AI technical operator

### 5. FL.ru

Best for: Russian-speaking freelance/IT buyers.

Publish profile and service offers:

- AI automation
- Web programming
- Chatbot development
- CRM/ERP automation
- Machine learning / AI services

## Tier 2 — Publish after Tier 1

### 6. Kwork

Best for: productized lower-ticket entry products.

Publish fixed-scope kworks:

- I will audit your AI-built repository and provide launch blockers
- I will create a Telegram hiring funnel for one vacancy

Use lower entry prices and upsell outside the first order according to platform rules.

### 7. Workspace.ru

Best for: Russian digital tenders and agency-style work.

Use for HR Autopilot and Telegram automation if you position as digital/product automation.

### 8. Indie Hackers / founder communities

Best for: Repo Rescue.

Publish as a founder-focused post:

- I help founders rescue AI-built MVPs before demo or launch

### 9. Product Hunt

Best later, when the page is polished as a product.

Launch not as a service only, but as a mini-product:

- AI Repo Rescue Diagnostic
- HR Autopilot Pilot

### 10. Telegram communities

Best for both offers if posted manually in relevant groups.

Do not spam. Use targeted posts and direct messages only when relevant.

## Publishing order

- Diagnostic
- Pilot

- Report
- Funnel
