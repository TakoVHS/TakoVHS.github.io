# HR Autopilot

## Telegram-first automation for mass hiring funnels

HR Autopilot helps teams reduce losses between a candidate response and an actual shift start.

It is designed for courier hiring, blue-collar hiring, production staff, operators, sewing teams and other high-volume roles.

## Problem

Many teams already pay for candidate traffic, but lose money because:

- candidates receive late replies;
- recruiters manage statuses manually;
- there is no clear funnel;
- the owner cannot see source performance;
- candidates disappear before call or shift;
- follow-up is inconsistent.

## Solution

A Telegram-first hiring funnel:

- candidate bot;
- screening form;
- status pipeline;
- recruiter handoff;
- reminders;
- source tracking;
- funnel report.
