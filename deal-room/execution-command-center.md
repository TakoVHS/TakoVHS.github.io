# Deal Room Execution Command Center

## Objective

Turn the sales pack into live listings, outbound conversations and paid diagnostics/pilots.

## Primary target for the first 7 days

1. Publish AI Repo Rescue Diagnostic on Upwork, Contra and LinkedIn.
2. Publish HR Autopilot on TenChat, Kwork and FL.ru.
3. Send 60 targeted direct messages.
4. Book at least 3 discovery calls.
5. Close at least 1 paid diagnostic or pilot.

## Source of truth files

- `index.html` — public landing page.
- `publish-upwork.md` — Upwork submission pack.
- `publish-contra.md` — Contra submission pack.
- `publish-linkedin.md` — LinkedIn submission pack.
- `publish-tenchat.md` — TenChat submission pack.
- `publish-kwork.md` — Kwork submission pack.
- `publish-flru.md` — FL.ru submission pack.
- `lead-tracker.csv` — lead and publication tracker.
- `quick-replies.md` — fast replies for incoming leads.

## Daily operating cadence

### Morning

- Publish or update one platform listing.
- Send 10 direct messages.
- Update `lead-tracker.csv`.

### Midday

- Reply to inbound messages.
- Push qualified leads to diagnostic/pilot.
- Book calls.

### Evening

- Review replies.
- Improve copy.
- Add notes to tracker.
- Prepare next-day target list.

## Deal stages

1. Not started
2. Message sent
3. Replied
4. Qualified
5. Call booked
6. Proposal sent
7. Paid
8. Delivery started
9. Delivered
10. Upsell offered

## First-week KPI

- 6 live platform listings/profiles.
- 60 direct messages.
- 10 replies.
- 3 calls.
- 1 paid diagnostic or pilot.

## Offer routing

If the lead is a founder, indie hacker, AI builder or agency: route to **AI Repo Rescue Diagnostic**.

If the lead is a recruiter, courier partner, HR agency, production owner or operations manager: route to **HR Autopilot Pilot**.

## Closing rule

Do not sell vague development hours. Push to a bounded paid entry product:

- Repo Diagnostic: $500–$1,500.
- HR Autopilot Pilot: 150,000 RUB.
