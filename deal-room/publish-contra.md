## Service 1

### Title

AI Repo Rescue Sprint for Founders

### Tagline

Turn your chaotic AI-built MVP into a verified launch candidate.

### Description

I help founders and small teams recover control over AI-built or contractor-built repositories that are hard to run, deploy or verify.

The sprint replaces uncertainty with evidence: technical audit, launch blockers, controlled fixes, smoke checks, run/deploy notes and founder handover.

Best fit:

- AI MVPs built fast but not verified;
- repositories that do not run reliably;
- demo or investor review deadlines;
- contractor handover problems;
- unclear deployment paths.

### Deliverables

- diagnostic report;
- launch blocker map;
- risk map;
- prioritized fix plan;
- run/deploy notes;
- handover summary.
