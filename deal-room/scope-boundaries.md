# Scope Boundaries

## Purpose

This document protects the offer from scope creep and makes the service easier to sell, deliver and defend.

## Repo Diagnostic

### Included

- Repository structure review.
- Setup and run-path review based on available documentation.
- Dependency and environment notes.
- Documentation review.
- Launch blocker identification.
- Risk map.
- Launch-readiness recommendation.
- Rescue sprint estimate.

### Not included

- Full feature development.
- Full product rewrite.
- Unlimited debugging.
- Long-term production support.
- New UI/UX design.
- Business strategy beyond technical launch-readiness.
- Work outside the reviewed repository unless agreed separately.

### Delivery format

- Written diagnostic report.
- Blocker map.
- Risk map.
- Recommended next-step plan.

## Rescue Sprint

### Included

- Agreed critical fixes.
- Run-path improvement.
- Smoke checks where relevant.
- README/runbook updates.
- Deployment notes.
- Handover summary.

### Not included

- New feature roadmap.
- Unlimited revisions.
- Full platform rebuild.
- Long-term monitoring.
- Customer support.
- Work not listed in the approved sprint scope.

## HR Autopilot Pilot

### Included

- One vacancy.
- One city.
- Candidate flow design.
- Screening questions.
- Status pipeline.
- Recruiter handoff model.
- Message templates.
- Basic funnel report.

### Not included

- Guaranteed hires.
- Paid traffic management.
- Full HR department replacement.
- Legal employment advice.
- Full CRM migration.
- Multi-city rollout unless agreed separately.

## Payment recommendation

- Diagnostic: 100% upfront.
- Rescue Sprint: 50% upfront, 50% before final handover.
- HR Pilot: 100% upfront or 70/30 depending on client trust.

## Sales rule

If a client asks for work outside the listed scope, convert it into a separate paid sprint, addendum or retainer.
